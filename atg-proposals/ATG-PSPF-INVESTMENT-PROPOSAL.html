Executive Summary

Alex Tsela Global (ATG) Holdings presents this investment proposal to the Public Service Pensions Fund (PSPF) for funding of a High-Value Agriculture Initiative in the Kingdom of Eswatini, focusing on two strategic pillars:

  1. Specialty Coffee Production — Arabica coffee cultivation
  2. Spring Water Bottling — Premium natural spring water processing

This initiative directly aligns with the Kingdom's national strategy to diversify away from traditional exports (sugar and timber) and the Prime Minister's directive to develop high-value crops for economic growth, job creation, and youth empowerment.

Why This Investment?

  • Government Priority: Coffee identified as strategic sector by PM Russell Dlamini
  • Import Substitution: Eswatini imports $2.32M coffee annually — local production reduces forex outflow
  • Guaranteed Off-take: Pre-arranged agreement with established buyer
  • Climate Suitable: Expert-verified sites ideal for Arabica cultivation
  • First-Mover Advantage: Early entrant in emerging specialty coffee sector
