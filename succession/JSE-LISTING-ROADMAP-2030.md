## Executive Summary

This document outlines the strategic roadmap for listing mSAPHOKATI TECHNOLOGIES on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange (JSE) by 2030. The company will operate with the discipline, governance, and transparency of a publicly listed company from Day One, ensuring readiness when the time comes to go public.

**Target:** JSE Main Board Listing by Q4 2030

---

## Part 1: JSE Listing Requirements

### Option A: JSE Main Board (Recommended for 2030)

The Main Board is for established companies with a proven track record. Requirements:

#### Financial Requirements
| Requirement | Minimum Threshold |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Share Capital** | R50 million (subscribed capital) |
| **Profit History** | Audited profit for 3 preceding years |
| **Minimum Profit** | R15 million pre-tax profit in last financial year |
| **Public Shareholding** | Minimum 20% of each class of shares held by public |
| **Number of Shareholders** | Minimum 300 public shareholders |
| **Market Capitalisation** | R500 million minimum (recommended: R1 billion+) |

#### Corporate Governance Requirements
| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Board Composition** | Minimum 4 directors (majority non-executive) |
| **Independent Directors** | At least 2 independent non-executive directors |
| **Board Committees** | Audit, Risk, Remuneration, Social & Ethics committees required |
| **Company Secretary** | Qualified company secretary with relevant experience |
| **Chairman** | Independent non-executive chairman |
| **CEO/CFO Separation** | CEO and CFO must be separate individuals |
| **Financial Director** | Suitably qualified and experienced |

#### Compliance Requirements
- Full compliance with Companies Act 71 of 2008
- King IV Corporate Governance Code compliance
- International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) compliant financials
- B-BBEE compliance and certificate
- SENS (Stock Exchange News Service) announcements
- Annual Integrated Report
- Sustainability/ESG Reporting

### Option B: AltX (Alternative Exchange) - Stepping Stone

AltX is designed for small to medium enterprises. Could be used as a stepping stone (list 2028, upgrade to Main Board 2030).

#### AltX Requirements (Less Stringent)
| Requirement | Minimum Threshold |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Share Capital** | R2 million |
| **Profit History** | Not required |
| **Public Shareholding** | 10% minimum |
| **Number of Shareholders** | Minimum 100 public shareholders |
| **Designated Adviser** | Mandatory (appointed from JSE-approved list) |

### Committee Structure

#### 1. Audit Committee
- **Composition:** Min 3 independent non-executive directors
- **Chair:** Independent director (not board chairman)
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Oversee financial reporting
  - Internal controls
  - External audit relationship
  - Risk management (financial)
  - Compliance monitoring

#### 2. Risk Committee
- **Composition:** Min 3 directors (majority non-executive)
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Enterprise risk management
  - IT governance
  - Cybersecurity oversight
  - Business continuity

#### 3. Remuneration Committee
- **Composition:** Min 3 non-executive directors (majority independent)
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Executive compensation
  - Incentive schemes
  - Non-executive director fees
  - Remuneration policy

#### 4. Social & Ethics Committee
- **Composition:** Min 3 directors/prescribed officers
- **Responsibilities:**
  - B-BBEE compliance
  - Environmental sustainability
  - Corporate citizenship
  - Labour practices
  - Ethics management

#### 5. Nominations Committee
- **Composition:** Min 3 directors (majority non-executive)
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Board appointments
  - Board diversity
  - Director independence assessment
  - Succession planning
