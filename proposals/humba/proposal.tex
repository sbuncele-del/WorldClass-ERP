\vspace{14pt}
{\fontsize{15pt}{15pt}\selectfont\latosemibold\color{hblack}\sectionnum{2}\;\;Brand Understanding}

\vspace{8pt}
{\color{textbody}\fontsize{10.5pt}{16pt}\selectfont
HUMBA\texttrademark{} is a \textbf{culture-driven premium streetwear} brand inspired by the snail --- a symbol of patience, resilience, and intentional movement across South African languages and cultures.

The brand speaks to a generation of \textbf{creatives, thinkers, and slow-living individuals} aged 8--35 who reject the rush and choose to move with meaning. HUMBA\texttrademark{} is not built for speed. It is built for purpose.}

\vspace{6pt}
\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=hblack, colframe=hblack, arc=4pt, boxrule=0pt, left=6mm, right=6mm, top=5mm, bottom=5mm]
{\color{hcream}\latolight\fontsize{14pt}{20pt}\selectfont\itshape
``Move at your own pace in a world that's always rushing.''}\\[6pt]
{\color{goldaccent}\latosemibold\fontsize{8pt}{8pt}\selectfont\addfontfeature{LetterSpace=6}--- HUMBA\texttrademark{} BRAND TAGLINE}
\end{tcolorbox}

\vspace{10pt}
{\color{textbody}\fontsize{10.5pt}{16pt}\selectfont
The community around the brand --- \textbf{The Snail Clan} --- connects the cultural significance of the snail across languages:}

\vspace{4pt}
\begin{center}
{\color{hbrown}\latosemibold\fontsize{9pt}{16pt}\selectfont
HUMBA\;\textbullet\;KHUMBA\;\textbullet\;UMNENKE\;\textbullet\;INKUMBA\;\textbullet\;KGOPA\;\textbullet\;KGOFU\;\textbullet\;KGOHU\;\textbullet\;SLAK\;\textbullet\;UMNENKHE\;\textbullet\;SGONOGONO}

\vspace{6pt}
{\color{textbody}\fontsize{10.5pt}{16pt}\selectfont
The website must be an extension of this identity: \textbf{calm, intentional, spacious, premium, and cultural}. Never loud. Never rushed. Never crowded.}
