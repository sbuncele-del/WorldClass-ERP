## Our Approach

We believe in doing this properly — not just filing returns to satisfy a deadline, but ensuring your tax profile is accurate, compliant, and that no unnecessary penalties or assessments arise from rushed or incomplete submissions.

To achieve this, we propose a **two-phase engagement**:

## Why Act Now

SARS has already issued a **Final Demand**. The 10-business-day window began on 14 April 2026. Continued non-compliance exposes you to:

- **Administrative penalties** — levied monthly per outstanding return (these accumulate and can be significant)
- **A formal summons**
- **Criminal prosecution** — carrying fines or imprisonment of up to two years

Early engagement gives us the best opportunity to manage your exposure and, where possible, apply for penalty remission.
