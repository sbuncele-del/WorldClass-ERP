3. Conversion

3.1 Equity Financing Conversion

Upon the closing of an Equity Financing:

(a) This Agreement shall automatically convert into shares of the same class issued in the Equity Financing;

(b) The number of shares issued shall equal the Investment Amount divided by the Conversion Price;

(c) The Investor shall execute any documents required to effect such conversion.

3.2 Liquidity Event Conversion

Upon a Liquidity Event:

(a) If the Liquidity Event occurs before an Equity Financing, the Investor shall receive, at the Investor's election:

(i) Cash payment equal to the Investment Amount; OR

(ii) A number of shares of Capital Stock equal to the Investment Amount divided by the Safe Price, immediately prior to the Liquidity Event.

(b) If the Investor elects shares, such shares shall be issued immediately prior to the Liquidity Event and shall participate in the proceeds of the Liquidity Event.

3.3 Dissolution Event

Upon a Dissolution Event:

(a) The Company shall pay the Investor an amount equal to the Investment Amount from the Company's remaining assets;

(b) Such payment shall be made before any distribution to ordinary shareholders but after payment of the Company's debts and obligations;

(c) If the Company's assets are insufficient, payment shall be made pro rata among SAFE holders and other creditors of similar priority.

Conversion Mechanics Summary

Example: Investor invests R500,000. SAFE converts at Series A (equity financing >R5M).
Safe Price = R15,000,000 ÷ Company Capitalization
Discount Price = Series A price × 80%
Investor gets more shares of whichever price is lower (better for investor).

At R10M pre-money, 120M shares: Safe Price = R15M ÷ 120M = R0.125/share
Investor receives: R500,000 ÷ R0.125 = 4,000,000 shares

4. Investor Rights

4.1 Information Rights

The Company shall provide the Investor with:

(a) Monthly management accounts within fifteen (15) days of each month end;

(b) Annual audited financial statements within ninety (90) days of the financial year end;

(c) Notice of any material developments affecting the business within seven (7) days of such occurrence.

4.2 Pro Rata Rights

The Investor shall have the right, but not the obligation, to participate in subsequent Equity Financings on a pro rata basis to maintain the Investor's percentage ownership of the Company.

4.3 Board Observer Rights

The Investor shall have the right to appoint one (1) observer to attend meetings of the Company's board of directors in a non-voting capacity, subject to reasonable confidentiality requirements.

4.4 Most Favored Nation

If the Company issues any other SAFE with terms more favorable to that investor, the Company shall promptly notify the Investor and provide the option to amend this Agreement to include such more favorable terms.
