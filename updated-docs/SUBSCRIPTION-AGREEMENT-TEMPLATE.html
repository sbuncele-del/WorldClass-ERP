A. The Company is engaged in the development and commercial deployment of SiyaBusa ERP — a fully integrated enterprise resource planning platform with 17+ cloud-based business modules and 150+ data tables, serving small to large enterprises across Southern Africa;

Article 4: Closing

4.1 Conditions Precedent

The obligations of the Parties are subject to the following conditions precedent being fulfilled by the Closing Date:

  1. Receipt of the Total Subscription Amount in the Company's bank account;
  2. Completion of FICA due diligence to the Company's satisfaction;
  3. Approval by the Company's board of directors of the share issuance;
  4. Execution of the Shareholders Agreement by the Subscriber.

4.2 Closing Obligations

Upon fulfilment of the Conditions Precedent, the Company shall:

  1. Issue the Subscription Shares to the Subscriber;
  2. Update the Company's share register to reflect the Subscriber's shareholding;
  3. Deliver a share certificate (or electronic equivalent) to the Subscriber.

Article 5: Representations and Warranties of the Company

The Company hereby represents and warrants to the Subscriber:

  1. Corporate Status: The Company is a validly existing company under South African company law;
  2. Authority: The execution of this Agreement has been duly authorised by the board of directors;
  3. Shares: The Subscription Shares, when issued, will be validly issued, fully paid and non-assessable;
  4. No Conflict: The Agreement does not conflict with the MOI or any other agreement of the Company;
  5. Litigation: There are no pending or threatened material legal proceedings affecting the Company;

Article 6: Representations and Warranties of the Subscriber

The Subscriber hereby represents and warrants to the Company:

  1. Legal Capacity: The Subscriber has full legal capacity to enter into this Agreement;
  2. No Violation: The execution of this Agreement does not violate any other agreement binding on the Subscriber;
  3. Investment Intent: The Subscriber is subscribing for investment purposes only and not for distribution;
  4. Risk Acknowledgement: The Subscriber is aware of and accepts the risks associated with investing in an early-stage technology company;
  5. Information: The Subscriber has had the opportunity to review the Company's business plan and financial projections, ask questions, and obtain information;
  6. Sophisticated Investor: The Subscriber has sufficient financial sophistication to evaluate the merits and risks of this investment.

Article 7: Covenants

7.1 Company Covenants

The Company covenants and undertakes to the Subscriber that:

  1. It will operate in accordance with the business plan shared with investors;
  2. It will maintain proper books of account and financial records;
  3. It will provide quarterly management accounts to investors within 30 days of quarter end;
  4. It will not issue new shares at a price below R0.10 per share without investor consent;
  5. It will use the subscription proceeds primarily for business growth.

7.2 Subscriber Covenants

The Subscriber covenants and undertakes to the Company that:

  1. It will execute and comply with the Shareholders Agreement;
  2. It will maintain strict confidentiality of all information received about the Company;
  3. It will not transfer the Subscription Shares except in compliance with the Shareholders Agreement and applicable law.
