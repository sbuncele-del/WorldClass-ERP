## Production Notes

### Video Specifications

| Platform | Resolution | Aspect Ratio | Max Length |
|:---------|:-----------|:-------------|:-----------|
| YouTube | 1920x1080 | 16:9 | No limit |
| LinkedIn | 1920x1080 | 16:9 or 1:1 | 10 min |
| Instagram Feed | 1080x1080 | 1:1 | 60 sec |
| Instagram Reels | 1080x1920 | 9:16 | 90 sec |
| TikTok | 1080x1920 | 9:16 | 3 min |
| Twitter | 1920x1080 | 16:9 | 2:20 |
| Facebook | 1920x1080 | 16:9 | 240 min |

### Brand Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: #1e3a8a (Deep Blue)
- Secondary: #7c3aed (Purple)
- Accent: #10b981 (Green)
- Background: #0f172a (Dark)

**Fonts:**
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular

**Music:**
- Upbeat, professional
- Royalty-free recommended sources: Epidemic Sound, Artlist

**Logo Usage:**
- Always include end card with logo
- Minimum clear space around logo
- Don't stretch or distort
