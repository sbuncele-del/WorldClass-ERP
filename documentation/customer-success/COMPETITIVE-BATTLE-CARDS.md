## How to Use Battle Cards

### Purpose
Battle cards help sales teams quickly understand competitive positioning and respond effectively when prospects mention competitors.

### Best Practices
- ✅ **DO** focus on customer value, not competitor bashing
- ✅ **DO** acknowledge competitor strengths where appropriate
- ✅ **DO** use specific, verifiable differentiators
- ✅ **DO** pivot to customer needs and outcomes
- ❌ **DON'T** make false claims about competitors
- ❌ **DON'T** share battle cards with prospects
- ❌ **DON'T** lead with competitor weaknesses

### Conversation Framework
```
1. ACKNOWLEDGE  - "Yes, [Competitor] is a well-known solution..."
2. DIFFERENTIATE - "Where SiyaBusa differs is..."
3. PROVE        - "For example, [Customer Name] chose us because..."
4. ASK          - "What's most important to you in this decision?"
```

## Battle Card: vs Sage

### Competitor Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Company** | Sage Group (UK-based, multinational) |
| **Products** | Sage 50, Sage 100, Sage 300, Sage X3, Sage Intacct |
| **Target Market** | SME to Mid-Market |
| **Pricing** | R500-R5,000/month depending on product |
| **Strengths** | Brand recognition, large user base, accountant familiarity |

### When You'll Encounter Sage
- Prospect using legacy Sage 50/Pastel
- Accountant recommendation
- Incumbent in established businesses

### Competitive Positioning

| Category | Sage | SiyaBusa | Advantage |
|----------|------|----------|-----------|
| **SARS Integration** | Manual/3rd party add-on | Built-in, automated | ✅ SiyaBusa |
| **Cloud Native** | Hybrid (legacy + cloud) | 100% cloud | ✅ SiyaBusa |
| **Industry Modules** | Generic | Healthcare, Mining, Construction | ✅ SiyaBusa |
| **Pricing Transparency** | Complex tiers | All-inclusive | ✅ SiyaBusa |
| **Brand Recognition** | High | Growing | ⚠️ Sage |
| **Accountant Familiarity** | Very high | Moderate | ⚠️ Sage |
| **Local Support** | Call centers | Direct team | ✅ SiyaBusa |

### Sage Weaknesses to Highlight
1. **Legacy Architecture** - Many Sage products are desktop-first with cloud added later
2. **SARS Manual Process** - Requires manual export/import for tax submissions
3. **Module Complexity** - Need multiple products for full ERP functionality
4. **Hidden Costs** - Implementation, training, support often extra
5. **Upgrade Path** - Moving between Sage products is painful

### Talking Points

**When prospect says: "We've always used Sage"**
> "Sage has been a trusted name for decades. Many of our customers came from Sage because they needed true cloud access, built-in SARS integration, and industry-specific features that Sage couldn't provide without expensive add-ons. Would you like to see how we compare for your specific needs?"

**When prospect says: "Our accountant uses Sage"**
> "We work seamlessly with external accountants. Our reports export in standard formats, and we can even give your accountant read-only access to review your books. Many accountants actually prefer SiyaBusa because the automated SARS submissions reduce their workload."

### Win Story
> "[Company Name] switched from Sage 300 to SiyaBusa and reduced their month-end close from 10 days to 3 days. Their SARS submissions that used to take hours are now automated. They're saving R50,000/year in software and support costs."

### When You'll Encounter QuickBooks

- Businesses that have outgrown spreadsheets

### Competitive Positioning

| Category | QuickBooks | SiyaBusa | Advantage |
|----------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Accounting** | Good | Comprehensive | ⚠️ Similar |

> "If your needs are truly simple, QuickBooks might work for now. Most businesses grow, though. When you need inventory management, employee payroll, or VAT submissions, you'll face a painful migration. SiyaBusa grows with you—start simple, add complexity as needed."

### Win Story
> "[Company Name] used QuickBooks for 3 years until they opened a second warehouse. They needed real-time inventory across locations and integrated payroll. Migration to SiyaBusa took 1 week, and they now manage 3 warehouses and 45 employees on one platform."
