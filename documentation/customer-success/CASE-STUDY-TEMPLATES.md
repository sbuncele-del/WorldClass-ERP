## Case Study Framework

### The STAR Method for Case Studies

Every SiyaBusa case study follows the STAR framework:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                         STAR METHOD                              │
├─────────────────┬───────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│   SITUATION     │  Background, context, company profile         │
├─────────────────┼───────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│   TASK          │  Challenges, pain points, goals               │
├─────────────────┼───────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│   ACTION        │  SiyaBusa implementation, modules used        │
├─────────────────┼───────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│   RESULTS       │  Measurable outcomes, ROI, benefits           │
└─────────────────┴───────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Key Metrics to Capture

| Category | Metrics |
|----------|---------|
| **Time Savings** | Hours saved per week/month, process time reduction |
| **Cost Reduction** | Operational costs, labor costs, error costs |
| **Revenue Impact** | Sales increase, faster invoicing, reduced DSO |
| **Compliance** | Audit time, penalty avoidance, submission accuracy |
| **Productivity** | Transactions processed, reports generated |

## Interview Questions

### Customer Interview Guide

Use these questions when interviewing customers for case studies:

#### Background Questions
1. Tell us about your company and what you do.
2. How many employees/locations do you have?
3. What was your business situation before SiyaBusa?

#### Challenge Questions
4. What were the main challenges you faced?
5. How did these challenges impact your business?
6. What solutions had you tried before?
7. Why weren't those solutions working?

#### Solution Questions
8. Why did you choose SiyaBusa ERP?
9. What modules are you using?
10. How was the implementation experience?
11. What features have been most valuable?

#### Results Questions
12. What measurable improvements have you seen?
13. How much time/money are you saving?
14. What can you do now that you couldn't before?
15. How has this impacted your team/customers?

#### Quote Questions
16. How would you describe SiyaBusa to a peer?
17. What would you say to someone considering SiyaBusa?
18. Would you be willing to be a reference?

## Design Guidelines

### Case Study Formatting

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Length** | 1-2 pages (800-1200 words) |
| **Format** | PDF, Web page |
| **Images** | Customer logo, team photo, screenshots |
| **Metrics** | Minimum 3 quantified results |
| **Quote** | At least 1 customer quote |

### Visual Elements

- Use data visualization for results
- Include before/after comparisons
- Feature customer logo prominently
- Use consistent SiyaBusa branding

### Approval Process

1. Draft case study from interview
2. Internal review (Marketing + CS)
3. Customer review and approval
4. Legal review (if needed)
5. Final design and publish
