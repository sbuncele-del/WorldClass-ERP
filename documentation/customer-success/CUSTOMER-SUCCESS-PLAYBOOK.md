## Introduction

### Purpose
This playbook provides Customer Success Managers (CSMs) with a comprehensive framework for ensuring SiyaBusa ERP customers achieve their desired outcomes, driving retention, expansion, and advocacy.

### Our Mission
> *"To transform every SiyaBusa customer into a success story by ensuring they maximize the value of our platform while achieving their business objectives."*

### What is Customer Success?
Customer Success is the proactive approach to ensuring customers achieve their desired outcomes while using SiyaBusa ERP. It's not just support—it's partnership.

## Onboarding Program

### 30-Day Onboarding Framework

#### Week 1: Foundation
| Day | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Welcome call & kickoff | CSM | Kickoff deck, success plan |
| 2 | Admin training session | CSM | Admin credentials, basic config |
| 3 | Data migration planning | Technical | Migration plan document |
| 4 | System configuration | Technical | Configured environment |
| 5 | User setup & permissions | Admin | User accounts created |

#### Week 2: Core Modules
| Day | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------|-------------|
| 6-7 | Financial module training | CSM | Chart of accounts setup |
| 8-9 | Inventory module training | CSM | Products & warehouses configured |
| 10 | Sales/CRM module training | CSM | Customer records imported |

#### Week 3: Advanced Features
| Day | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------|-------------|
| 11-12 | HR/Payroll setup | CSM | Employee records, payroll config |
| 13-14 | Compliance modules (SARS Sentinel) | CSM | Tax profiles configured |
| 15 | Reporting & dashboards | CSM | Custom reports created |

#### Week 4: Go-Live
| Day | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|-----|----------|-------|-------------|
| 16-18 | User acceptance testing | Customer | UAT sign-off |
| 19-20 | Final adjustments | Technical | Issues resolved |
| 21 | Go-live preparation | CSM | Go-live checklist complete |
| 22 | **GO-LIVE** | All | System live |
| 23-30 | Hypercare support | CSM | Daily check-ins |

### Onboarding Success Criteria

✅ All core modules configured  
✅ Data migration complete and validated  
✅ Key users trained (minimum 80% attendance)  
✅ At least 3 transactions processed successfully  
✅ Customer satisfaction score ≥ 8/10  
✅ No critical open issues

## Health Scoring

### Customer Health Score Model

The health score predicts churn risk and identifies intervention opportunities.

#### Health Score Components

| Factor | Weight | Data Source | Scoring |
|--------|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Product Usage** | 30% | Analytics | Daily active users, feature adoption |
| **Support Health** | 20% | Helpdesk | Ticket volume, resolution time, escalations |
| **Engagement** | 20% | CRM | Meeting attendance, email response |
| **Payment Health** | 15% | Finance | On-time payments, outstanding invoices |
| **NPS/Sentiment** | 15% | Survey | Latest NPS score, feedback |

#### Health Score Calculation

```
Health Score = (Usage × 0.30) + (Support × 0.20) + (Engagement × 0.20) 
             + (Payment × 0.15) + (NPS × 0.15)
```

#### Health Score Ranges

| Score | Status | Color | Action |
|-------|--------|-------|--------|
| 85-100 | Healthy | 🟢 Green | Maintain, identify expansion |
| 70-84 | Stable | 🟡 Yellow | Monitor closely, proactive outreach |
| 50-69 | At Risk | 🟠 Orange | Intervention required |
| 0-49 | Critical | 🔴 Red | Executive escalation |

## Risk Management

### Early Warning Signs

Watch for these churn indicators:

| Signal | Risk Level | Response |
|--------|------------|----------|
| No login in 7+ days | 🟡 Medium | Friendly check-in email |
| No login in 14+ days | 🟠 High | Phone call required |
| No login in 30+ days | 🔴 Critical | Executive escalation |
| Support escalation | 🟡 Medium | CSM joins support call |
| Payment 30+ days late | 🟠 High | Finance + CSM intervention |
| Negative NPS feedback | 🟡 Medium | Follow-up within 24 hours |
| Key contact leaves | 🟠 High | Emergency relationship rebuild |
| Competitor mention | 🔴 Critical | Executive engagement |

### Risk Intervention Playbook

#### Level 1: Yellow Alert (Score 70-84)
1. Send personalized email within 48 hours
2. Offer training refresher session
3. Share relevant success story or tip
4. Schedule health check call within 1 week

#### Level 2: Orange Alert (Score 50-69)
1. Phone call within 24 hours
2. Create action plan with specific goals
3. Weekly check-ins until score improves
4. Involve technical team if needed
5. Offer incentives (extended support, free training)

#### Level 3: Red Alert (Score 0-49)
1. Immediate phone call (within 4 hours)
2. Escalate to CS Director
3. Executive sponsor engagement
4. Create 30-day rescue plan
5. Consider pricing/contract adjustments
6. Daily check-ins during recovery

### Save Plays

| Situation | Save Play |
|-----------|-----------|
| Price objection | Demonstrate ROI, offer payment plan |
| Missing features | Roadmap preview, custom development quote |
| Poor support experience | Executive apology, dedicated support |
| Champion left company | Rapid relationship building with successor |
| Competitor evaluation | Executive meeting, loyalty discount |

## Expansion & Upsell

### Expansion Opportunities

#### Module Expansion Matrix

| If Using | Recommend | Value Proposition |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Financial only | HR/Payroll | Unified employee costs, automated payroll |
| Inventory only | Sales/CRM | Complete order-to-cash cycle |
| Sales/CRM only | Inventory | Real-time stock visibility |
| Core modules | Compliance | Automated SARS submissions |
| All standard | Industry verticals | Healthcare, Mining, Construction |

#### Expansion Triggers

| Trigger | Opportunity | Timing |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Growing headcount | HR/Payroll module | When hiring 10+ employees |
| Multi-location expansion | Warehouse Management | New branch opening |
| VAT registration | SARS Sentinel | Registration confirmed |
| Audit concerns | Audit Shield | Year-end approaching |
| International expansion | Multi-currency | Cross-border sales starting |

### User Expansion

| Current Users | Upsell Trigger | Approach |
|---------------|----------------|----------|
| 1-5 users | Department requests access | Offer user bundles |
| 5-15 users | Wait times on licenses | Unlimited user tier |
| 15+ users | Multiple departments | Enterprise tier |

### Expansion Conversation Framework

```
1. DISCOVER
   "I noticed your team has grown to 25 employees. How are you 
   managing payroll currently?"

2. CONNECT
   "Many of our customers in your situation have found that 
   integrated payroll saves 10+ hours per month."

3. DEMONSTRATE VALUE
   "Would you like to see how ABC Manufacturing reduced their 
   payroll processing time by 60%?"

4. PROPOSE
   "Based on your needs, I'd recommend adding our HR/Payroll 
   module. Here's what that would look like..."

5. SECURE COMMITMENT
   "Should we schedule a demo with your HR team next week?"
```

## Renewal Process

### Renewal Timeline

```
120 Days Before    90 Days Before    60 Days Before    30 Days Before    Renewal
     │                  │                  │                  │             │
     ▼                  ▼                  ▼                  ▼             ▼
  Health           Renewal          Negotiation        Contract         Close
  Assessment       Conversation     (if needed)        Delivery         Deal
```

### Renewal Checklist

#### 120 Days Before Renewal
- [ ] Review customer health score
- [ ] Compile usage analytics
- [ ] Document value delivered (ROI metrics)
- [ ] Identify any open issues
- [ ] Prepare renewal proposal

#### 90 Days Before Renewal
- [ ] Schedule renewal conversation
- [ ] Present business review with value summary
- [ ] Discuss upcoming needs and roadmap
- [ ] Introduce renewal terms
- [ ] Identify expansion opportunities

#### 60 Days Before Renewal
- [ ] Address any concerns or objections
- [ ] Finalize pricing and terms
- [ ] Send renewal contract
- [ ] Follow up on contract status

#### 30 Days Before Renewal
- [ ] Confirm contract signature
- [ ] Process payment or payment plan
- [ ] Update CRM with renewal details
- [ ] Celebrate and thank customer

### Renewal Conversation Script

```
"Hi [Name], as we approach your renewal, I wanted to take a moment 
to review the value SiyaBusa has delivered this year:

• You've processed [X] transactions worth R[Y] million
• Your team has saved approximately [Z] hours on manual tasks
• SARS submissions have been 100% on-time

I'm excited to continue this partnership. For the coming year, 
we're proposing [renewal terms]. 

Is there anything you'd like to discuss before we proceed?"
```
