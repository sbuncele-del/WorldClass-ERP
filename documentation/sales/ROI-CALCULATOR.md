## ROI Overview

### Why ROI Matters

Investing in ERP is a significant decision. This calculator helps quantify the financial impact of implementing SiyaBusa ERP, demonstrating clear return on investment through:

- **Cost Reduction** - Lower software, labor, and operational costs
- **Productivity Gains** - Time savings across the organization
- **Revenue Growth** - Better decisions, faster quotes, improved cash flow
- **Risk Mitigation** - Compliance, audit readiness, reduced errors

### ROI Formula

```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs × 100

Example:
ROI = (R500,000 - R150,000) / R150,000 × 100 = 233%
```
