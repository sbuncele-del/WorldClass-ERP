## Our Pricing Model

### Platform Fee + Per-User Fee

SiyaBusa ERP follows the global ERP industry standard:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                                 │
│   MONTHLY COST = PLATFORM FEE + (USER COUNT × PER-USER FEE)    │
│                                                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Components:**

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Platform Fee** | Base monthly cost for the software platform |
| **Per-User Fee** | Monthly cost for each active user |
| **Implementation** | One-time setup and training costs |

**What's Included in Platform Fee:**
- Software access and updates
- South African cloud hosting
- Standard storage allocation
- Base support level
- SARS compliance tools
- Automatic backups

**What's Included in Per-User Fee:**
- Full module access (per tier)
- User account and authentication
- Mobile access
- Email notifications
- Personal dashboards

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Pricing Questions

**Q: Why do you use platform fee + per-user pricing?**
A: This is the global ERP industry standard used by SAP, Oracle, Microsoft, and Sage. It makes it easy to compare vendors and scale costs predictably with your business.

**Q: Are there any hidden costs?**
A: No. Your total monthly cost is Platform Fee + (Users × Per-User Fee). All modules for your tier are included. Implementation and training are one-time costs.

**Q: Can I add users mid-month?**
A: Yes. Additional users are pro-rated for the remainder of the month.

**Q: What happens if I go below minimum users?**
A: You'll still be charged for the minimum user count for your plan.

### Plan Questions

**Q: Can I upgrade my plan?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, with costs pro-rated.

**Q: Can I downgrade my plan?**
A: Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. 30 days notice required.

**Q: What's the contract term?**
A: Monthly billing is month-to-month. Annual billing requires 12-month commitment with early termination fees.

### Module Questions

**Q: Do I need to pay extra for modules?**
A: No. All modules included in your tier are available at no extra cost.

**Q: Can I add individual Enterprise modules to a Professional plan?**
A: Contact sales for module add-on pricing if you need specific Enterprise modules without upgrading.
