## FAQs

### What is Founding Member pricing?
R1,499/month for 10 users — all 17 modules included. Price locked for 12 months. Limited to 50 spots.

### What happens after the first year?
Standard pricing applies (R299/user/month is the first-year rate). Founding members receive preferential rates and at least 30 days notice before any price change.

### Are there any hidden fees?
No. No platform fees, no module add-ons, no implementation consultant fees. The price you see is the price you pay (plus VAT).

### Can I add more users?
Yes. Additional users beyond your plan are billed at R299/user/month.

### Is there a contract?
Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Annual billing is available at a discount.

### Do you offer migration support?
Yes. For Founding Members, migration from other platforms is included at no extra charge.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. AES-256 encryption, South African hosted infrastructure, automated backups, and high-availability architecture.
