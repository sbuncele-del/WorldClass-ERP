## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Discovery (30 minutes)

**Goals:**
- Understand current pain points
- Qualify budget and timeline
- Identify decision makers

**Key Questions:**

**About Their Current Situation:**
- "What system are you using today?"
- "What's working well? What's frustrating?"
- "How much time do you spend on month-end?"
- "How do you handle SARS correspondence?"

**About Their Goals:**
- "What would success look like in 12 months?"
- "What triggered you to look at new systems now?"
- "What's the cost of not solving this problem?"

**About Decision Process:**
- "Who else is involved in this decision?"
- "What's your timeline for making a change?"
- "What budget range are you working with?"

---

### Stage 2: Demo (60 minutes)

**Before the Demo:**
- Confirm attendees and their roles
- Understand their key pain points
- Prepare relevant industry examples

**Demo Structure:**

| Segment | Duration | Focus |
|:--------|:---------|:------|
| **Opening** | 5 min | Confirm agenda, goals |
| **Company Overview** | 5 min | Brief, credibility |
| **Core Demo** | 30 min | Their pain points |
| **Differentiators** | 10 min | SARS Sentinel, Audit Shield |
| **Q&A** | 10 min | Address concerns |

**Key Demo Flows:**

**For Financial Teams:**
Dashboard → GL Entry → Bank Reconciliation → Trial Balance → SARS Sentinel

**For Operations Teams:**
Dashboard → Inventory → Purchase Order → Receiving → Stock Report

**For HR Teams:**
Dashboard → Employee List → Leave Request → Payrun → Payslip → IRP5

**Always Show:**
- Clean, modern interface
- SARS Sentinel (unique!)
- Audit Shield (unique!)
- Mobile responsiveness

---

### Stage 3: Proposal (24-48 hours after demo)

**Proposal Contents:**
1. Executive Summary
2. Solution Overview
3. Implementation Plan
4. Investment Summary
5. ROI Analysis
6. Next Steps

**Pricing Presentation:**

| Component | Description | Monthly |
|:----------|:------------|:--------|
| Platform Fee | Core system, updates, hosting | R8,000 - R15,000 |
| User Licenses | Per active user | R650 - R1,500 |
| Implementation | One-time setup (amortize if needed) | Quote |
| Training | Included in platform | R0 |
| Support | Included in platform | R0 |

**Always Include:**
- Total monthly cost
- Annual total
- Comparison to current state
- ROI calculation
- Payment terms

---

### Stage 4: Negotiation

**Common Objections & Responses:**

**"It's too expensive"**
> "I understand budget is important. Let me ask—what's the cost of your current inefficiencies? Our clients typically save 10-20 hours per month on month-end alone. At R500/hour loaded cost, that's R5,000-R10,000 in savings monthly. When you factor in reduced audit fees, faster collections, and fewer errors, most clients see positive ROI within 6 months."

**"We're too small for an ERP"**
> "That's exactly why we built SiyaBusa. Traditional ERPs are overkill for growing businesses. We scale with you—start with what you need today, add more as you grow. Many of our happiest clients started with just 5 users."

**"Implementation takes too long"**
> "Traditional ERPs, yes. SiyaBusa is different. We're cloud-based with pre-built SA configurations. Most clients are live in 2-4 weeks. We handle the data migration, you handle the business."

**"We've invested too much in our current system"**
> "I hear that a lot. Let me ask—is your current system helping you grow, or holding you back? The real cost isn't what you've spent, it's the opportunity cost every month. We offer flexible transitions—run parallel if you want—to make the switch painless."

**"My accountant/auditor uses Pastel"**
> "Great news—SiyaBusa exports standard formats that work with any accountant. Many of our clients' accountants love us because our audit trail and reports are cleaner than what they got before. We're happy to demo for your accountant if that helps."

---

### Stage 5: Close

**Buying Signals:**
- Asking about implementation timeline
- Involving more stakeholders
- Asking for references
- Discussing specific use cases
- Negotiating terms

**Closing Techniques:**

**Summary Close:**
> "Based on our conversations, you need [pain point 1], [pain point 2], and [pain point 3]. We've shown how SiyaBusa solves all of these for [price]. What questions do you have before we move forward?"

**Next Step Close:**
> "The next step is a 30-minute call with your team to answer any final questions and schedule your kickoff. Does Tuesday or Wednesday work better?"

**Urgency Close (use sparingly):**
> "We have implementation capacity starting in [timeframe]. If we sign by [date], we can have you live by [date], well before your year-end."

### Discount Authority

| Discount | Approval Needed |
|:---------|:----------------|
| Up to 10% | Sales Rep |
| 11-20% | Sales Manager |
| 21-30% | Director |
| 30%+ | CEO |

### When to Discount

✅ **Good reasons:**
- Multi-year commitment (10-15%)
- Large user count (volume)
- Strategic account (reference, industry)
- Competitive situation

❌ **Avoid discounting for:**
- "Just because"
- Without getting something in return
- Early in the sales process
- To close faster
