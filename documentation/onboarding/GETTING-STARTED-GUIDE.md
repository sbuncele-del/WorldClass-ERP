## Step 1: Accessing SiyaBusa

### Web Browser Access

1. **Open your browser** (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox recommended)
2. **Go to:** Your company's SiyaBusa URL (provided by your administrator)
3. **Bookmark the page** for quick access

### Mobile Access

SiyaBusa works on tablets and smartphones:
- Responsive design adapts to your screen
- Full functionality on mobile browsers
- No app installation required

---

## Step 2: Logging In

### Your Login Credentials

Your administrator will provide:
- **Email Address:** Your work email
- **Temporary Password:** Set during account creation

### First-Time Login

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your temporary password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be prompted to change your password

### Password Requirements

Your new password must have:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter
- At least 1 number
- At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)

## Step 4: Navigating Modules

### The Side Menu

Click any module to open it:

| Module | What It Does |
|:-------|:-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview, KPIs, quick actions |
| **Financial** | Accounting, invoices, payments |
| **HR & Payroll** | Employees, leave, payslips |
| **Inventory** | Stock, warehouses, transfers |
| **Sales & CRM** | Customers, quotes, orders |
| **Purchasing** | Suppliers, purchase orders |
| **Manufacturing** | BOMs, work orders, production |
| **Reports** | Custom reports, analytics |
| **Compliance** | SARS, audits, regulatory |
| **Settings** | Your preferences, company setup |

### Module Structure

Each module follows the same pattern:

```
Module Name
├── List View (see all records)
├── Create New (add records)
├── Search & Filter (find specific items)
└── Reports (module-specific reports)
```

## Step 5: Common Tasks

### Creating a New Record

1. Navigate to the relevant module
2. Click the **"+ New"** or **"Create"** button
3. Fill in the required fields (marked with *)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Editing a Record

1. Find the record in the list view
2. Click on the record to open it
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Save"**

### Searching for Records

1. Use the search bar at the top of any list
2. Type your search term
3. Press Enter or click the search icon
4. Use filters to narrow results

### Exporting Data

1. Navigate to any list view
2. Click the **"Export"** button
3. Choose format (Excel, PDF, CSV)
4. Download your file

## Step 6: Getting Help

### In-App Help

- **?** icon - Context-sensitive help
- **Tooltips** - Hover over icons for descriptions
- **Guided Tours** - Step-by-step walkthroughs

### Support Channels

| Channel | Best For | Response Time |
|:--------|:---------|:--------------|
| **Knowledge Base** | How-to guides, FAQs | Instant |
| **Email Support** | Complex issues | 4-8 hours |
| **Live Chat** | Quick questions | During business hours |
| **Phone Support** | Urgent issues | Immediate (Premium) |

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|:---------|:-------|
| `Ctrl + /` | Open search |
| `Ctrl + N` | Create new record |
| `Ctrl + S` | Save current record |
| `Esc` | Close dialog/cancel |
