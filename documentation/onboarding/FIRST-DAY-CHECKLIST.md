# SiyaBusa ERP - First Day Checklist

## Quick Reference Card for New Users

---

## ✅ Before You Start

☐ **Received login credentials** from your administrator  
☐ **Know your company's SiyaBusa URL**  
☐ **Have a modern browser** (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox)  
☐ **Blocked 30 minutes** for initial setup

---

## 🔐 Account Setup (10 minutes)

### Step 1: First Login
☐ Go to your company's SiyaBusa URL  
☐ Enter your email address  
☐ Enter your temporary password  
☐ Click "Sign In"

### Step 2: Change Password
☐ Create a strong password (8+ chars, uppercase, number, symbol)  
☐ Confirm your new password  
☐ Click "Update Password"

### Step 3: Complete Profile
☐ Add your full name  
☐ Upload a profile photo (optional)  
☐ Add your phone number  
☐ Set your preferred language  
☐ Set your timezone  

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Orientation (10 minutes)

### Explore the Interface
☐ **Find the side menu** (left side)  
☐ **Locate the notification bell** (top right)  
☐ **Find your profile menu** (top right)  
☐ **Identify the search bar** (top center)

### Check Your Access
☐ **Count your available modules** (side menu)  
☐ **Note which modules are greyed out** (no access)  
☐ **Identify your primary module** (where you'll work most)

### Review Your Dashboard
☐ **Read any welcome messages**  
☐ **Check for pending tasks**  
☐ **Review any notifications**

---

## 🔧 Personalization (5 minutes)

### Notification Settings
☐ Go to Profile → Settings → Notifications  
☐ Enable email notifications for important alerts  
☐ Set notification frequency preferences

### Display Preferences
☐ Choose your preferred theme (light/dark)  
☐ Set your date format (DD/MM/YYYY recommended for SA)  
☐ Set your number format (1 000,00 for SA)

### Security (Recommended)
☐ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)  
☐ Review your active sessions

---

## 📱 Mobile Access (2 minutes)

### Test Mobile Access
☐ Open SiyaBusa URL on your phone  
☐ Verify you can log in  
☐ Bookmark or add to home screen
