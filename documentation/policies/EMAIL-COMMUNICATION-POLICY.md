# Email Communication Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes standards for all email communications sent on behalf of Masaphokati Technologies (Pty) Ltd and SiyaBusa ERP. It ensures consistency, professionalism, and compliance with applicable regulations.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees and contractors
- All business email communications
- Marketing emails and newsletters
- Transactional emails (invoices, receipts, notifications)
- Support communications
- Internal company emails

---

## 3. Email Signature Standards

### 3.1 Standard Email Signature

All business emails must include a professional signature:

```

[Job Title] | Masaphokati Technologies

📧 [email]@siyabusa.co.za
📞 [phone number]
🌐 siyabusa.co.za

SiyaBusa ERP — We Rule. We Govern. We Empower.

────────────────────────────────────
This email and any attachments are confidential and intended solely 
for the addressee. If you received this in error, please delete it 
and notify the sender.
```

### 3.2 Signature Elements

| Element | Required | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Full name | ✅ Yes | As registered in HR |
| Job title | ✅ Yes | Official title only |
| Company name | ✅ Yes | "Masaphokati Technologies" |
| Email | ✅ Yes | Company email only |
| Phone | Optional | Work number preferred |
| Website | ✅ Yes | siyabusa.co.za |
| Tagline | ✅ Yes | Brand tagline |
| Confidentiality notice | ✅ Yes | Required for external emails |

### 3.3 Prohibited in Signatures

- ❌ Personal quotes or sayings
- ❌ Political statements
- ❌ Religious content
- ❌ Animated GIFs
- ❌ Large images (>50KB)
- ❌ Personal social media links
- ❌ Unauthorized logos or badges

## 4. Professional Email Standards

### 4.1 Subject Lines

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|-------|---------|
| Be specific and descriptive | Use vague subjects like "Hello" or "Question" |
| Include reference numbers when relevant | Leave subject blank |
| Keep under 60 characters | Use ALL CAPS |
| Indicate urgency appropriately | Mark everything as urgent |

**Good examples:**
- "Invoice #1234 - January 2026"
- "SiyaBusa ERP: Your trial expires in 3 days"
- "Meeting request: Q1 Planning - Jan 15"

### 4.2 Email Body

**Opening:**
- Use professional greetings: "Dear [Name]," or "Hello [Name],"
- Avoid overly casual: "Hey" or "Yo"

**Body:**
- Get to the point quickly
- Use short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for lists
- One topic per email when possible

**Closing:**
- Professional sign-offs: "Kind regards," "Best regards," "Thank you,"
- Avoid: "Cheers," "Later," "TTYL"

### 4.3 Tone and Language

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|-------|---------|
| Be professional and courteous | Be overly casual or use slang |
| Proofread before sending | Send with typos or errors |
| Use proper grammar | Use text speak (u, ur, thx) |
| Be concise | Write unnecessarily long emails |
| Respond within 24 business hours | Leave emails unanswered |

## 5. Email Types and Standards

### 5.1 Marketing Emails

**Requirements:**
- Clear sender identification (Masaphokati Technologies / SiyaBusa ERP)
- Physical address in footer
- Unsubscribe link (one-click)
- Compliance with POPIA and CAN-SPAM

**Template elements:**
- Header with logo
- Clear value proposition
- Single call-to-action
- Footer with contact info and unsubscribe

### 5.2 Transactional Emails

**Types:**
- Account confirmations
- Password resets
- Invoice/receipt notifications
- System alerts
- Support ticket updates

**Requirements:**
- Immediate delivery
- Clear subject indicating purpose
- No promotional content (keeps deliverability high)
- Reply-to address that is monitored

### 5.3 Support Emails

**Standards:**
- Acknowledge receipt within 4 hours (business hours)
- Include ticket/reference number
- Set clear expectations for resolution time
- Follow up until resolved
- Request feedback after closure

### 5.4 Internal Emails

**Guidelines:**
- Use appropriate distribution lists
- Don't "Reply All" unless necessary
- Mark confidential information clearly
- Use @mentions for specific attention needed

---

## 6. Confidentiality and Security

### 6.1 Confidential Information

- Never send passwords via email
- Use secure file sharing for sensitive documents
- Encrypt attachments containing personal data
- Verify recipient before sending confidential information

### 6.2 Data Protection (POPIA Compliance)

- Only collect email addresses with consent
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 5 business days
- Never share customer email lists
- Include privacy policy link in marketing emails
- Retain email records per retention policy

### 6.3 Phishing Prevention

- Never click suspicious links
- Verify unusual requests via phone
- Report suspected phishing to IT immediately
- Don't forward suspicious emails

---

## 7. Email Lists and Newsletters

### 7.1 Subscription Management

- Double opt-in required for marketing lists
- Clear description of what subscribers will receive
- Easy unsubscribe process
- Segment lists appropriately

### 7.2 Frequency Guidelines

| List Type | Frequency |
|-----------|-----------|
| Newsletter | Monthly |
| Product updates | As needed (max weekly) |
| Promotional | Max 2 per month |
| Transactional | As triggered |

### 7.3 Content Standards

- Valuable, relevant content
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clear calls-to-action
- Proper alt-text for images
- Plain text version available

---

## 8. Response Time Standards

| Email Type | Response Time |
|------------|---------------|
| Customer support | Within 4 business hours |
| Sales inquiries | Within 2 business hours |
| Partner communications | Within 24 business hours |
| Internal emails | Within 24 business hours |
| Urgent (marked) | Within 1 business hour |

---

## 9. Out-of-Office Messages

### 9.1 Required Elements

```
Thank you for your email.

I am currently out of the office from [date] to [date] with limited 
access to email.

For urgent matters, please contact [Name] at [email].

I will respond to your email upon my return.

Kind regards,
[Your Name]
Masaphokati Technologies
```

### 9.2 Guidelines

- Set before leaving
- Include return date
- Provide alternative contact for urgent matters
- Keep message professional and brief

---

## 10. Prohibited Email Practices

The following are strictly prohibited:

1. **Personal use** of company email for non-work purposes
2. **Forwarding** company emails to personal accounts
3. **Sending** offensive, discriminatory, or harassing content
4. **Sharing** confidential information without authorization
5. **Mass emailing** without approval from marketing
6. **Impersonating** another employee or entity
7. **Subscribing** company emails to external lists without approval
8. **Auto-forwarding** to external addresses

---

## 11. Email Retention

| Email Type | Retention Period |
|------------|------------------|
| General correspondence | 3 years |
| Financial/contractual | 7 years |
| Legal matters | Permanent (or as advised) |
| Marketing campaigns | 3 years |
| Support tickets | 5 years |

---

## 12. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Verbal warning
- Written warning
- Suspension of email privileges
- Disciplinary action up to termination
- Legal action if applicable

---

## 13. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed. Employees will be notified of significant changes.
