## Quick Reference

### For Sales Team
When onboarding new customers, provide:
1. Terms of Service (all customers)
2. Privacy Policy (all customers)
3. Master Services Agreement (enterprise customers)
4. Service Level Agreement (reference for support tiers)

### For Legal Review
Key documents for contract negotiation:
1. Master Services Agreement (main contract)
2. Data Processing Agreement (POPIA compliance)
3. Service Level Agreement (SLA terms)

### For Compliance
POPIA compliance documentation:
1. Privacy Policy
2. Data Processing Agreement
3. Terms of Service (data sections)
