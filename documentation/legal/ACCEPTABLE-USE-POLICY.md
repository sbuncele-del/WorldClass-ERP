## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") defines the acceptable and prohibited uses of the SiyaBusa ERP service ("Service") provided by Masaphokati Technologies (Pty) Ltd ("Provider", "we", "us"). This Policy protects our customers, users, systems, and the broader internet community.

### 1.2 Applicability

This Policy applies to all users of the Service, including:
- Customer organizations
- Authorized users
- Administrators
- Anyone accessing the Service through a customer's account

### 1.3 Agreement

By using the Service, you agree to comply with this Policy. Violation may result in suspension or termination of access.

## 2. General Use Standards

### 2.1 Lawful Use

You must use the Service only for lawful purposes. You must comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including:

- South African law
- Data protection laws (including POPIA)
- Tax laws and SARS requirements
- Industry-specific regulations
- International laws applicable to your operations

### 2.2 Authorized Use

You may use the Service only:
- For your legitimate business purposes
- As described in the Documentation
- In accordance with your subscription terms
- Within your authorized user limits

### 2.3 Account Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- All activities under your account
- Maintaining the security of login credentials
- The actions of all authorized users
- Compliance with this Policy by your users

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

You may NOT use the Service to:

| Prohibited Activity | Examples |
|---------------------|----------|
| **Violate laws** | Fraud, money laundering, tax evasion |
| **Infringe rights** | Copyright, trademark, or patent infringement |
| **Harm individuals** | Harassment, stalking, threatening behavior |
| **Discriminate** | Based on race, gender, religion, etc. |
| **Facilitate crime** | Aid any criminal activity |

### 3.2 Harmful Content

You may NOT use the Service to store, process, or transmit:

- Malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Content that is defamatory or libelous
- Content that is obscene or pornographic
- Content promoting violence or hatred
- Content that infringes intellectual property rights
- Personally identifiable information obtained unlawfully

### 3.3 Security Violations

You may NOT:

| Prohibited Activity | Description |
|---------------------|-------------|
| **Unauthorized access** | Access systems or data not intended for you |
| **Circumvent security** | Bypass security measures or access controls |
| **Probe vulnerabilities** | Scan or test for security weaknesses |
| **Interfere with service** | Disrupt the Service or its infrastructure |
| **Reverse engineer** | Decompile, disassemble, or reverse engineer the Service |
| **Data extraction** | Use automated means to scrape or extract data |

### 3.4 Network Abuse

You may NOT:

- Transmit spam or unsolicited commercial email
- Conduct phishing or social engineering attacks
- Participate in denial of service attacks
- Forge headers or manipulate identifiers
- Harvest email addresses or personal information
- Send communications that disguise their origin

### 3.5 System Abuse

You may NOT:

- Exceed usage limits or quotas
- Attempt to overload systems
- Use excessive bandwidth or storage
- Run unauthorized automated processes
- Store data unrelated to the Service's intended purpose
- Use the Service for cryptocurrency mining

### 3.6 Commercial Violations

You may NOT:

- Sublicense, resell, or redistribute the Service
- Share login credentials with third parties
- Use the Service to provide services to third parties (without authorization)
- Remove or alter proprietary notices
- Use Provider's trademarks without permission

If you process regulated data (healthcare, financial services, etc.), you must:
- Comply with industry-specific requirements

- Implement appropriate controls

### 8.1 Monitoring

We may monitor use of the Service to:
- Ensure compliance with this Policy
- Protect the Service and users
- Comply with legal requirements
- Improve the Service

### 8.2 Violations

If we become aware of a potential violation:

1. **Investigation:** We will investigate the reported violation
2. **Notification:** We may notify you of the concern
3. **Response:** We may require you to take corrective action
4. **Suspension:** We may suspend access pending investigation
5. **Termination:** Serious violations may result in termination

| Severity | Examples | Consequence |
|----------|----------|-------------|
