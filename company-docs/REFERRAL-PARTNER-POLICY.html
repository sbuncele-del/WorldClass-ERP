Pulling Together, Growing Together

"Masaphokati" — At the center, connecting all

At Masaphokati Technologies, we believe in the power of relationships and collective growth. Our Referral Partner Program is built on the principle of pulling each other together — when you help us succeed, we ensure you share in that success.

1. Program Overview

1.1 Purpose

This Referral Partner Policy establishes the terms and conditions under which individuals and organizations ("Referral Partners") can earn commissions by introducing new clients to Masaphokati Technologies' products and services.

1.2 Philosophy

We recognize that:

  • Business growth happens through trusted relationships
  • Those who open doors deserve to benefit from the opportunities created
  • Long-term partnerships are built on fair, transparent reward structures
  • Success should be shared with those who help make it possible

2. Commission Structure

2.1 Standard Commission Rate

Element Rate
Referral Commission 10% of Contract Value
Payment Timing Upon each invoice payment received
Duration For the lifetime of the referred client relationship

2.2 How It Works

Client Payment → Masaphokati Receives Funds → 10% Commission Paid to Referrer

Example:

  • Referred client signs a contract worth R 100,000/month
  • Client pays their monthly invoice of R 100,000
  • Referral Partner receives R 10,000 commission
  • This continues for every payment made by the referred client

2.3 Commission Calculation

Contract Type Commission Basis Example
Monthly Subscription 10% of each monthly payment R5,000/month → R500/month commission
Annual Contract 10% of each payment received R60,000/year → R6,000/year commission
Project Fee 10% of each milestone payment R200,000 project → R20,000 total commission
Implementation 10% of implementation fees R50,000 setup → R5,000 commission

3. Eligibility & Qualification

3.1 Who Can Be a Referral Partner?

  • Existing clients of Masaphokati Technologies
  • Business professionals and consultants
  • Industry associations and networks
  • Technology partners and integrators
  • Individual introducers with relevant business networks
  • Organizations aligned with our values

3.2 Qualified Referral Requirements

For a referral to qualify for commission, the Referral Partner must:

  1. Introduce — Make a formal introduction between Masaphokati and the prospective client
  2. Facilitate — Actively assist in the sales process where needed
  3. Support — Help navigate any challenges until the deal closes
  4. Register — Submit the referral through our official process before client contact

3.3 Exclusions

The following do not qualify for referral commissions:

  • Clients who approach Masaphokati directly without introduction
  • Clients already in discussion with Masaphokati
  • Self-referrals (referring your own company)
  • Referrals made after client has already engaged with us

4. Referral Process

4.1 Step-by-Step Process

Step Action Responsibility
1 Register Referral Partner submits referral via email/form
2 Acknowledgment Masaphokati confirms receipt within 48 hours
3 Introduction Partner introduces Masaphokati to prospect
4 Sales Process Masaphokati handles proposal and negotiation
5 Partner Support Partner assists as needed through close
6 Contract Signed Client signs agreement with Masaphokati
7 Invoicing Begins Client is invoiced for services
8 Commission Paid Partner receives 10% upon each payment

4.2 Referral Registration

To register a referral, submit the following information:

Required Information:

  • Referral Partner name and contact details
  • Prospective client company name
  • Contact person name and title
  • Contact email and phone number
  • Brief description of the opportunity
  • Estimated contract value (if known)

Submit to: referrals@masaphokati.co.za

4.3 Referral Protection Period

  • Initial Period: 90 days from referral registration
  • Extension: If actively engaged, protection extends until decision
  • Expiration: If no engagement within 90 days, referral expires

5. Payment Terms

5.1 Commission Payment Schedule

Event Commission Payment
Client pays invoice Commission calculated
Within 14 days of receipt Commission paid to Partner

5.2 Payment Methods

  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) to South African bank accounts
  • International wire transfer for non-SA partners
  • Payment made in the currency of the contract

5.3 Documentation Required

Before first commission payment, Partners must provide:

  • Completed W-8BEN or tax documentation (international)
  • South African ID/Passport copy
  • Banking details for payment
  • Signed Referral Partner Agreement

5.4 Tax Responsibility

  • Referral Partners are responsible for their own tax obligations
  • Masaphokati will issue payment summaries for tax purposes
  • Commission payments may be subject to withholding tax requirements

6. Partner Responsibilities

6.1 Expected Conduct

Referral Partners agree to:

✅ Represent Masaphokati Technologies professionally and accurately

✅ Not make false claims about our products or services

✅ Disclose the referral relationship to prospective clients when appropriate

✅ Maintain confidentiality of any sensitive information shared

✅ Act in good faith throughout the referral process

✅ Support the sales process constructively

6.2 Prohibited Activities

Referral Partners must NOT:

❌ Guarantee specific outcomes or results to prospects

❌ Negotiate pricing or contract terms on behalf of Masaphokati

❌ Access or share confidential client information

❌ Represent themselves as employees of Masaphokati

❌ Engage in any unethical or illegal activities

7. Masaphokati Responsibilities

7.1 Our Commitments

We commit to:

✅ Process referrals promptly and professionally

✅ Keep Partners informed of referral status (within confidentiality limits)

✅ Pay commissions accurately and on time

✅ Provide Partners with necessary sales materials

✅ Treat referred prospects with excellence

✅ Maintain transparency in all dealings

7.2 Support Provided

  • Product information and brochures
  • Demo access for qualified referrals
  • Sales support and proposal assistance
  • Regular commission statements
  • Partner portal access (coming soon)

8. Term & Termination

8.1 Agreement Term

  • This agreement is effective upon Partner registration
  • Continues until terminated by either party
  • Commission rights continue for existing referrals post-termination

8.2 Termination

Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

Post-Termination:

  • Partner continues to receive commissions on existing referred clients
  • No commissions for new referrals after termination
  • Commission continues as long as referred client remains active

8.3 Termination for Cause

Masaphokati may terminate immediately if Partner:

  • Violates this policy
  • Engages in fraudulent activity
  • Damages Masaphokati's reputation
  • Breaches confidentiality

9. Confidentiality

9.1 Confidential Information

Partners agree to keep confidential:

  • Client information and contract details
  • Pricing and commercial terms
  • Business strategies and plans
  • Any proprietary information shared

9.2 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination indefinitely.

10. General Provisions

10.1 Independent Contractor

Referral Partners are independent contractors, not employees, agents, or representatives of Masaphokati Technologies.

10.2 Non-Exclusivity

This agreement is non-exclusive. Partners may refer to other companies, and Masaphokati may have multiple referral partners.

10.3 Modifications

Masaphokati reserves the right to modify this policy with 30 days notice to active Partners.

10.4 Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

10.5 Dispute Resolution

Disputes shall be resolved through good faith negotiation, followed by mediation if necessary.
