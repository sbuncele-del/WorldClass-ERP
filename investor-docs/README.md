## 📤 How to Share

### WhatsApp
1. Open WhatsApp on your phone or desktop
2. Select the contact or group
3. Click the attachment icon (📎)
4. Select "Document"
5. Browse to this folder and select the PDF(s)
6. Send!

### Email
- Attach the PDFs directly to your email
- All PDFs are professionally formatted with headers and footers
- Documents include proper branding and page numbers
