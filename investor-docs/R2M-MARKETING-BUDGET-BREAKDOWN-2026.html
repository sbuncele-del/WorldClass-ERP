Executive Summary

SiyaBusa ERP is a fully built, production-deployed cloud ERP platform with 25+ modules, 400+ API endpoints, and native South African compliance (SARS, BCEA, POPIA, B-BBEE). The technology is complete. What we need now is a commercial engine to take it to market.

This R2,000,000 seed investment will be deployed across 12 months to fund three things: (1) a dedicated sales team to actively hunt and close SME clients, (2) marketing infrastructure — merchandise, banners, digital campaigns, and trade show presence — to build visibility where South African SME decision-makers gather, and (3) back-office support staff to onboard, train, and retain every client we sign.

54% of the budget funds people directly (5 hires, phased). The remaining 46% funds the tools they need, the brand collateral they carry, and the events they attend. Commission for sales staff is paid from generated revenue — not from this budget — ensuring alignment between spend and outcomes.

Every price in this document is sourced from a named South African supplier, published SaaS pricing page, or recognised salary benchmark. A full source reference appendix is included at the end of this document.

Commission Structure — Self-Funded from Revenue (Not from this Budget)

Performance Bonus: 10% of Year 1 contract value per signed deal, paid on receipt of client’s first payment

Pricing: R399/user/month, all-inclusive. Average client = 20 users = R7,980/month

Example: Signing a 20-user client (R7,980/mo × 12 = R95,760/yr) = R9,576 commission per BDE

Quarterly Target: Each BDE targets 3 clients/quarter → 24+ new clients across both BDEs in 12 months

Revenue if target met: 36 clients × R7,980 avg/mo = R263,340 MRR → R3.16M ARR by Month 12

Commission is OTE paid from generated revenue — not from this R2M budget. This ensures BDE incentives are directly aligned with growth.

Key Takeaway

At R399/user/month, even the conservative scenario reaches R1.72M ARR with a 6.3:1 LTV:CAC ratio — well above the 3:1 SaaS gold standard.

In the base case, 36 clients generate R3.16M ARR by Month 12 with an 80% gross margin. The CAC payback is just 3 months. Technology risk is zero — what we are buying is customers.

Key Assumptions

  • All prices are exclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated. VAT at 15% will apply to physical goods and SA-domiciled services.
  • USD-denominated SaaS tools converted at an indicative exchange rate of R18.00 : USD 1.00 (April 2026 mid-rate).
  • Salary figures are Cost to Company (CTC) — inclusive of basic salary, UIF, SDL, and company contributions. Take-home pay differs by individual tax profile.
  • BDE commission (10% of Year 1 contract value) is paid from generated revenue, not from this R2M capital allocation.
  • Monthly churn assumption: <2% per month, consistent with B2B SaaS industry benchmarks for SA mid-market.
  • Customer Lifetime Value assumes 4-year average retention — conservative for enterprise ERP.
  • Marketing CPC and CPM rates reflect Google Keyword Planner and LinkedIn Campaign Manager SA data as at April 2026.
  • Event stand fees are based on published exhibitor prospectus pricing for 2026 where available, or 2025 pricing +6% CPI escalation.
  • Contingency reserve (12.5%) is deliberately held unallocated, to be deployed based on mid-year performance review.
