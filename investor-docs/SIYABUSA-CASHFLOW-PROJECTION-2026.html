10 — Key Financial Assumptions
Pricing model R399/user/month, flat rate, all-inclusive. No discounts modelled. First 2 clients may receive a 30-day pilot at no charge.
Average users per client 20 users assumed across the portfolio. Range in practice: 5 (micro SME) to 200+ (enterprise).
Client churn Zero churn modelled in Year 1 (ERP is a deeply embedded system; switching cost is high). Year 2 onward: <2% monthly churn applied.
AWS infrastructure cost Base R8,000/month fixed + R8/active user variable. Includes EC2 (Auto Scaling), RDS PostgreSQL, Redis, S3, CloudFront, ALB, Route53, CloudWatch. FX rate: R18.50/USD. A 10% ZAR depreciation adds ~R900/month at M12.
Payment processing Peach Payments (SA-native): 2.5% transaction fee on MRR. Monthly debit order collection.
Support staff (COGS) M1–M6: 1 customer support agent @ R25,000/month. M7–M11: 2 agents @ R35,000/month total. M12+: 3 agents @ R45,000/month total. Direct service delivery — classified as COGS, not OpEx.
Dev / Founders (OpEx) 2 founders drawing R43,000/month combined throughout Year 1. Engineering headcount grows in Year 2 (R120K/month).
Sales staff (OpEx) First sales hire in Month 4 @ R18,000/month. Upgraded or second hire from Month 7 @ R25,000/month.
Marketing spend (OpEx) R15,900/month recurring drawn from the R2M marketing budget allocation. Event costs drawn separately as they occur.
Seed capital deployment R2,000,000 received Month 0. 16.67% equity issued. Pre-money valuation R10,000,000. No debt, no further raise modelled until break-even is achieved.
Taxation No income tax in Year 1 (operating at a loss). 28% corporate tax applied from Year 2 on EBITDA after assessed loss carry-forward.
LTV calculation basis LTV = Avg MRR per client (R7,980) × 12 months × 4-year retention × 75.4% gross margin = R574,560. CAC R45,000, payback 5.6 months.
