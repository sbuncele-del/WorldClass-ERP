## 3. Pricing Model: Per-User SaaS Pricing

### Core Principle: Every User Pays

We use **per-user, per-month pricing** — the industry standard for ERP/SaaS. No free tier. Every user on the platform contributes to covering our infrastructure costs.

### Trial Period (NOT Free Tier)

| Trial | Duration | What's Included |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| **Free Trial** | 14 days | Full access, 3 users max |
| **Extended Trial** | 30 days | For serious prospects, requires call |

**After trial:** Convert to paid or data is archived. No indefinite free usage.

### Per-User Pricing Structure

**Standard Rate: R980 per user per month**

This rate ensures each user covers their infrastructure cost + provides healthy margin.
