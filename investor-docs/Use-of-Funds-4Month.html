Risk Mitigation

What if first customer takes longer?

Scenario: No customer by May 2026

Mitigation:

  • R47,000 contingency extends runway by ~5 weeks
  • Reduce marketing spend 50% (extends 2 weeks)
  • Reduce operational costs (extends 2 weeks)
  • Revenue from implementation services (R7,500-R25,000 per customer)
  • Total runway extension possible: ~2 months

What if AWS costs spike?

Scenario: Unexpected traffic or usage

Mitigation:

  • AWS billing alerts set at 80% of budget
  • Auto-scaling with cost limits
  • Reserved instances if predictable load
  • Contingency buffer covers 2x spike

What if marketing doesn't work?

Scenario: Low lead quality or high CAC

Mitigation:

  • Pause underperforming channels within 2 weeks
  • Reallocate to channels showing ROI
  • Pivot to outbound/cold outreach (lower cost)
  • Leverage beta partner referrals
