## 💡 Answer These Objections

### "Your company is too small"
"Fair. Two points: 1) Every big company started small. 2) We're not asking you to bet the farm—just test us in 10 stores. If we can't handle 10, we can't handle 1,500. But if we nail it, you've found a gem while we're still affordable."

### "How do you integrate with our existing systems?"
"We're API-first. We've integrated with POS, WMS, banking APIs, e-commerce platforms. For Shoprite, we'd conduct a 2-week assessment, then build custom connectors. Typical timeline: 4-6 weeks."

### "What if you go out of business?"
"1) Source code escrow. 2) Your data is portable. 3) Open architecture. 4) We're profitable with 500+ customers, growing 300% YoY. The risk is we get acquired by Microsoft—in which case, you benefit."

### "Cloud isn't secure"
"Is your on-premise setup ISO 27001 certified? Do you have a DR site? When was your last restore test? Cloud is MORE secure. That's why FNB and Standard Bank moved to the cloud."

### "Can it scale to 1,500 locations?"
"Yes. Multi-tenant architecture, database sharding, load balancing. We'd start with 10 stores to prove performance, then roll out region by region. Smart, low-risk approach."
