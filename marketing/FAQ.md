## 🇿🇦 South African Compliance

### Q: Is AetherOS SARS-compliant?

**A:** Yes! We support all major SARS submissions:
- ✅ EMP201 (Monthly employer reconciliation)
- ✅ EMP501 (Annual reconciliation)
- ✅ IRP5/IT3(a) (Employee tax certificates)
- ✅ VAT201 (VAT returns)
- ✅ IT14 (Income tax returns)
- ✅ Direct eFiling integration

### Q: Does it calculate PAYE, UIF, and SDL automatically?

**A:** Yes! 
- **PAYE:** Uses current SARS tax tables, auto-updates
- **UIF:** 2% (1% employee + 1% employer)
- **SDL:** 1% of payroll
- All calculations are automatic and compliant.

### Q: Is it BCEA-compliant for leave?

**A:** Yes! Built-in BCEA leave rules:
- Annual leave: 21 days per year
- Sick leave: 30 days per 3-year cycle
- Family responsibility: 3 days per year
- Maternity leave: 4 consecutive months
- All automatically accrued and tracked

### Q: Does it support Employment Equity reporting?

**A:** Yes! We track:
- EE Act compliance
- EEA2 & EEA4 report generation
- Employment equity plans
- B-BBEE scorecard tracking

### Q: What about POPIA compliance?

**A:** AetherOS is POPIA-compliant:
- ✅ Data encryption (256-bit)
- ✅ Access controls and audit trails
- ✅ Data retention policies
- ✅ Right to be forgotten
- ✅ Consent management
- ✅ Data processing agreements
- ✅ ISO 27001 certified data centers
