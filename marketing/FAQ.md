## 🇿🇦 South African Compliance

### Q: Is SiyaBusa SARS-compliant?

**A:** Yes! Full SARS integration is built-in, not an add-on:

- ✅ **EMP201** — Monthly employer reconciliation
- ✅ **EMP501** — Annual reconciliation
- ✅ **IRP5/IT3(a)** — Employee tax certificates
- ✅ **VAT201** — VAT returns
- ✅ **IT14** — Income tax returns
- ✅ **Direct eFiling** — Submit directly to SARS

Our **SARS Sentinel AI** also:
- Tracks all compliance deadlines
- Sends automated reminders
- Validates data before submission
- Provides audit trail documentation

---

### Q: Does it calculate PAYE, UIF, and SDL automatically?

**A:** Yes! All calculations are automatic and always up-to-date:

| Tax | Calculation | Updates |
|:----|:------------|:--------|
| **PAYE** | Current SARS tax tables | Auto-updated annually |
| **UIF** | 2% (1% employee + 1% employer) | Automatic |
| **SDL** | 1% of payroll | Automatic |

You never have to manually calculate or worry about being out of date.

### Q: Is it BCEA-compliant for leave?

| Leave Type | Entitlement | How We Handle It |
