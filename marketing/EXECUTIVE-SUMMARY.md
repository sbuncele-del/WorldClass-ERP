- **Implementation** typically takes 6-18 months and costs R300K-R800K

### The Market Size
- **60,000+** South African businesses in the mid-market segment
- **$800M+** ERP market in South Africa
- **15%** annual growth in cloud ERP adoption

### Our Solution
SiyaBusa ERP offers enterprise-grade functionality at **40-50% lower cost** than global competitors, with South African compliance built-in from day one.
