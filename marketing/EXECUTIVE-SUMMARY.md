## The Problem

South African businesses face unique challenges:
- **Complex Tax Compliance:** SARS eFiling, PAYE, UIF, SDL, VAT, B-BBEE requirements
- **Labor Law Complexity:** BCEA, LRA, Employment Equity Act, Skills Development Act
- **Legacy Systems:** Using multiple disconnected software tools (QuickBooks + Excel + manual SARS submissions)
- **High Cost:** SAP/Oracle solutions cost R50K-R500K+ monthly, out of reach for SMEs
- **Local Support Gap:** International ERPs lack South African-specific features

**Result:** 63% of SA businesses report compliance challenges, 47% face SARS penalties annually

---

## The Solution: AetherOS ERP

A **100% South African-compliant ERP** designed specifically for local businesses:

### Core Value Propositions

1. **RSA Compliance Built-In (Not Added On)**
   - Automatic SARS eFiling integration (EMP201, EMP501, VAT201, IT14)
   - BCEA leave calculations, PAYE/UIF/SDL automated
   - B-BBEE scorecard tracking
   - Employment Equity reporting

2. **Enterprise Features at SME Pricing**
   - R999/month starter (10 users) vs. SAP R50K/month
   - Full modules: Financial, HR, Sales, Purchase, Inventory, Manufacturing, Warehouse, Assets, SARS Sentinel
   - No per-user fees after 10 users

3. **Built for South Africa**
   - ZAR currency, SA ID validation, +27 phone formats
   - SA public holidays, RSA banking integration
   - Local language support (English, Afrikaans, Zulu, Xhosa planned)
   - South African tax years, SARS deadlines calendar

4. **Modern Technology Stack**
   - Cloud-hosted (AWS Cape Town region)
   - Mobile-responsive React frontend
   - Real-time dashboards and KPIs
   - AI-powered SARS Sentinel for compliance monitoring
