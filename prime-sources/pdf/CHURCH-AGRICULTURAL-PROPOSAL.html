Prime Sources is a professional agricultural consulting firm based in Mankayane, Manzini, in the Kingdom of eSwatini. We specialize in helping organizations establish and manage profitable agricultural projects from conception to completion.

THE CHALLENGE FACING CHURCHES TODAY

We understand that many churches face significant financial challenges:

  • Declining tithes and offerings due to economic pressures on congregants
  • Growing operational costs including utilities, maintenance, and staff
  • Limited funding for community outreach and development programs
  • Dependence on giving alone which is often insufficient and unpredictable
Giving alone is no longer enough. Churches need sustainable income-generating projects that can supplement offerings and create long-term financial stability.
