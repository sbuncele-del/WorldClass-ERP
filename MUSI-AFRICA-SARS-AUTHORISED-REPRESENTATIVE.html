1. BACKGROUND

1.1  The Company is a private company duly incorporated and registered in terms of the Companies Act, No. 71 of 2008, under registration number 2016/367070/07.

1.2  The Company is required to register with the South African Revenue Service ("SARS") and to activate its tax profile for the purposes of compliance with all applicable tax legislation.

1.3  In terms of Section 246 of the Tax Administration Act, No. 28 of 2011 ("the Act"), a company is required to appoint a natural person as its representative taxpayer and public officer, who shall be responsible for performing any duties imposed on the company under the Act.

2. RESOLUTION

It is hereby resolved that:

2.1  Mmakhotso Evellyn Sithole (ID: 8203150962084), in her capacity as Director of the Company, be and is hereby appointed as the Authorised Representative and Public Officer of the Company for all purposes relating to the South African Revenue Service, with immediate effect from 15 April 2026.

2.2  The Authorised Representative and Public Officer shall be empowered to perform the following functions on behalf of the Company:

(a) To activate and manage the Company's SARS eFiling profile;
(b) To register the Company for all applicable tax types, including but not limited to Income Tax, Value-Added Tax (VAT), Pay-As-You-Earn (PAYE), Skills Development Levy (SDL), and Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF);
(c) To submit tax returns, declarations, and any other documents required by SARS on behalf of the Company;
(d) To receive correspondence, assessments, and notices from SARS on behalf of the Company;
(e) To lodge objections and appeals on behalf of the Company;
(f) To engage with SARS officials and attend to any queries, audits, or verifications on behalf of the Company;
(g) To act as the Public Officer of the Company in terms of Section 246(1) of the Act and to perform all duties and obligations imposed on the Public Officer by law; and
(h) To perform any and all acts necessary in connection with the Company's tax affairs at SARS.
