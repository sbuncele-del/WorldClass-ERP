## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **Keep your login details safe.** Your CSD username and password are confidential.
3. **Update your profile regularly.** If any details change (directors, bank account, address, B-BBEE), log in and update your CSD profile.
4. **Registration does NOT guarantee work.** Being on the CSD means you are eligible to be considered — actual procurement follows separate tender/quotation processes.
