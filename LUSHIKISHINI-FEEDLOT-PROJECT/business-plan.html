Project Description

What We Do

The project operates a cattle feedlot business using a batch feeding and rotational sales model:

Step 1: Purchase

Buy weaner calves (180-220 kg) at market prices (~E6,500 each)

Step 2: Feed

Intensive feeding for 90-120 days using crop residues and supplements

Step 3: Sell

Sell finished cattle (380-450 kg) to abattoirs at ~E14,000 each

Step 4: Repeat

Continue cycle with new batch - selling 10 cattle every month
