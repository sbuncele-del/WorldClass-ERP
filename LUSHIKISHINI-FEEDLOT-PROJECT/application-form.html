5 PROJECT DESCRIPTION
Describe the project activities in detail:

The Lushikishini Cattle Feedlot Cooperative will establish a commercial cattle feedlot operation with the following activities:

  • Purchase: Acquire weaner cattle (180-220 kg) from local farmers at approximately E6,500 each
  • Feeding: Intensive feeding program for 90-120 days using locally sourced crop residues and supplements
  • Infrastructure: Construct 3 additional feedlots (10 cattle each) to complement our existing kraal
  • Sales: Sell finished cattle (380-450 kg) to registered abattoirs at approximately E14,000 each
  • Cycle: Continuous monthly sales cycle of 10 cattle with replacement purchases
Objectives and Expected Outcomes:
  • Generate sustainable income for 10 member households (~E54,000 annually per member)
  • Create employment within the Lushikishini community
  • Contribute to food security through local beef production
  • Develop local capacity in commercial livestock farming
  • Establish a replicable model for community-based agricultural enterprise
