QUICK REFERENCE: WHAT EADF IS LOOKING FOR

They Want to See How You Demonstrate It
You’re a real farmer Photos of existing feedlot, cattle, infrastructure
You can repay the loan Cash flow projection showing repayment capacity
You fall under priority value chains Beef = priority livestock commodity ✅
You’ve done your homework 3 quotes per item, business plan, market letters
You’re properly registered AIIS, cooperative registration, industry association
You have skin in the game Own contribution (existing infrastructure worth E150,000+)
The project is viable Technical expertise (BSc Agric on team), proven operations

IMPORTANT REMINDERS

  1. EADF is a LOAN, not a grant — you must repay it from business cash flows
  2. All documents must be CERTIFIED — uncertified copies will be rejected
  3. 3 quotes per item — this is strict, don’t skip any
  4. AIIS registration is COMPULSORY — do this first, it takes time
  5. The resolution to borrow is CRITICAL — without it, the coop cannot legally take a loan
  6. Keep copies of EVERYTHING you submit
