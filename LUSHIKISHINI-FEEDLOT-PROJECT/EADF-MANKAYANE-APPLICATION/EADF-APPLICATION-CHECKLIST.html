⚠ IMPORTANT REMINDERS

  1. EADF is a LOAN, not a grant — you must repay from business cash flows
  2. All documents must be CERTIFIED — uncertified copies will be rejected
  3. 3 quotes per item is strict — each on supplier letterhead with date and prices
  4. AIIS registration is COMPULSORY — do this first, it may take time to process
