3. Paste the **System Prompt** below into the agent's instructions
4. Add these **Actions** to the agent:
   - **Facebook Pages: Create Page Post**
   - **Schedule by Zapier** (trigger: every day at 08:00 SAST)

5. Set the trigger: **Schedule — every weekday at 08:00** (UTC+2)
6. Connect your **Siyabusa ERP** Facebook page
