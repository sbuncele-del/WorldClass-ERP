## PART G: DISTINCTIVENESS ARGUMENT

### Why AUDIT SHIELD is Registrable

1. **Arbitrary Combination:** While "audit" and "shield" are common words, their combination in relation to software creates a distinctive mark.

2. **Suggestive Rather Than Descriptive:** The mark suggests protection in audit contexts but does not directly describe the software's functionality.

3. **No Common Usage:** The term "AUDIT SHIELD" is not commonly used in the software industry as a generic term.

4. **Brand Investment:** The applicant has invested in building brand recognition around this term.

5. **Consumer Perception:** Users associate "AUDIT SHIELD" specifically with the applicant's audit management solution.
