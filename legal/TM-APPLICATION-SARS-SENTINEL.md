## PART G: LEGAL CONSIDERATIONS

### Use of "SARS" in Trademark

**Legal Analysis:**

1. **Descriptive Use:** The term "SARS" is used in a descriptive manner to indicate that the software product relates to South African Revenue Service compliance matters.

2. **No Official Affiliation:** The mark does not suggest official endorsement by or affiliation with the South African Revenue Service.

3. **Composite Mark:** The mark "SARS SENTINEL" is a composite mark where the distinctiveness comes from the combination and particularly from the word "SENTINEL."

4. **Disclaimer:** A disclaimer has been included to clarify that no exclusive rights are claimed over "SARS" separately.

5. **Precedent:** Similar composite marks using government agency acronyms with descriptive additions have been registered in various jurisdictions.

### Risk Mitigation

- Clear marketing materials stating no government affiliation
- Prominent disclaimer on all product materials
- Focus on "SENTINEL" as the distinctive element
- Alternative fallback mark: "TAX SENTINEL" if objection arises
