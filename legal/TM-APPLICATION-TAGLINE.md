## DISTINCTIVE CHARACTER

### Argument for Registration

1. **Unique Combination:** The specific combination of "We Rule. We Govern. We Empower." is original and distinctive.

2. **Brand Association:** The tagline has been consistently used in connection with SiyaBusa ERP products and services.

3. **Commercial Impression:** The tagline creates a strong commercial impression linking it to the applicant's business software services.

4. **No Descriptive Function:** The tagline does not directly describe the goods/services but rather conveys a brand message about empowerment and control.

5. **Consumer Recognition:** Through consistent use, the tagline has acquired distinctiveness in the marketplace.
