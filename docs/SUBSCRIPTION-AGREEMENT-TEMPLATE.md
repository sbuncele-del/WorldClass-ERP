# 5. Representations and Warranties

## 5.1 Company Representations

The Company represents and warrants to the Subscriber that:

### 5.1.1 Corporate

- The Company is duly incorporated and validly existing
- The Company has the power to enter into this Agreement
- This Agreement has been duly authorized
- The execution does not breach any law or agreement

### 5.1.2 Share Capital

- The authorized share capital is 1,000,000,000 Ordinary Shares
- All issued Shares are fully paid and validly issued
- The Subscription Shares will be validly issued

### 5.1.3 Business

- The Company owns all material Intellectual Property
- There is no material litigation pending or threatened
- The Company is compliant with all material laws

### 5.1.4 Financial

- The financial information provided is accurate
- There are no undisclosed material liabilities
- No material adverse change has occurred

## 5.2 Subscriber Representations

The Subscriber represents and warrants to the Company that:

### 5.2.1 Capacity

- The Subscriber has full legal capacity to enter into this Agreement
- This Agreement has been duly authorized
- The Subscriber is not insolvent or subject to winding-up

### 5.2.2 Investment

- The Subscriber is subscribing for investment purposes
- The Subscriber has sufficient funds to pay the Subscription Amount
- The source of funds is lawful
- The Subscriber understands the risks of the investment

### 5.2.3 Information

- The Subscriber has received all information required
- The Subscriber has had opportunity to ask questions
- The Subscriber is not relying on any representation not contained herein

### 5.2.4 Sophisticated Investor

The Subscriber confirms that they:
- Have sufficient knowledge and experience in business and financial matters
- Are capable of evaluating the merits and risks of this investment
- Can bear the economic risk of total loss of this investment

## 8.1 Lock-Up Period

The Subscriber agrees not to Transfer any Shares for a period of **12 months** from the Closing Date without prior written consent of the Company and Founders.

## 8.2 Shareholders Agreement

All Transfers are subject to the terms of the Shareholders Agreement.

# 9. Confidentiality

## 9.1 Obligation

Each Party agrees to keep confidential:
- The terms of this Agreement
- All information received regarding the other Party
- All business information of the Company

## 9.2 Exceptions

This obligation does not apply to information that:
- Is or becomes public (other than through breach)
- Is required to be disclosed by law
- Is disclosed to professional advisors under confidentiality
