# Risk Factors

## Financial Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|:-----|:-------|:-----------|
| Slower customer acquisition | Lower revenue, extended runway | Conservative projections, milestone-based hiring |
| Higher churn than expected | Lower LTV, more acquisition needed | Invest in customer success early |
| Price pressure | Lower ARPA | Value-based selling, feature differentiation |
| Longer sales cycles | Higher CAC | Focus on ready-to-buy customers |

## Mitigation Strategy

1. **Monthly financial reviews** with investors
2. **Milestone-based hiring** - only hire when metrics justify
3. **Conservative cash management** - 6-month runway minimum
4. **Flexible cost structure** - contractors before FTEs where possible
