## 🎯 **What is AetherOS ERP?**

**AetherOS** is a next-generation, cloud-native Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system that combines the best design principles from **Oracle, SAP, and Microsoft** into a unified, world-class platform.

### **Core Capabilities:**
- 💰 **Financial Management** - Double-entry accounting, multi-currency, trial balance, P&L
- 🛒 **Sales & CRM** - Customer management, quotations, invoicing, pipeline tracking
- 📦 **Inventory Management** - Stock control, warehouses, transfers, valuations
- 👥 **HR & Payroll** - Employee records, leave management, payroll processing
- 🏭 **Manufacturing** - Bill of materials, work orders, production tracking
- 🏦 **Cash Management** - Bank reconciliation, cashflow forecasting, treasury
- ⚖️ **Practice Management** - Professional services, time tracking, client billing
- 🇿🇦 **SARS Sentinel** - South African tax compliance and e-filing
