## Key Points to Mention

When talking to your friend, make sure to cover:

### What We Built
- ✅ Complete ERP system (not just an idea)
- ✅ 25+ modules (Finance, HR, Inventory, Manufacturing, etc.)
- ✅ Native South African compliance (SARS, BCEA, POPIA)
- ✅ AI assistants for natural language queries
- ✅ Actually deployed and working on AWS

### What We Need Help With

**Documentation Specialist Role:**
- Write user guides for each module
- Create video script outlines
- Develop quick-start guides
- Build help center content
- Make technical features understandable

**Compliance Assistant Role:**
- Document our PAYE/UIF/SDL calculation methods
- Prepare SARS eFiling integration application
- Research SARS API requirements
- Help with regulatory correspondence
- Track compliance deadlines

### What's In It For Them
- Part-time, flexible hours
- Work from anywhere
- Paid position (R5,000-R10,000/month)
- Get in early on something big
- Learn about ERP/fintech industry
- Potential for growth when company succeeds

### Timeline
- Funding discussions: Now - February 2026
- Positions start: Once funding secured (target: February/March 2026)
- Beta program: January - March 2026
- Public launch: April 2026

## Personalization Tips

Replace [Friend's Name] and add personal touches:

**If they have documentation/writing experience:**
> "Your ability to explain complex things simply is exactly what we need. Our system is powerful but we need someone who can make it accessible to business owners who aren't tech-savvy."

**If they have accounting/compliance experience:**
> "Your background in [accounting/tax/compliance] would be invaluable. We've built the calculations, but we need someone who understands the regulatory side to help us get SARS approval."

**If they're looking for flexible work:**
> "I know you mentioned wanting more flexible work. This is part-time, work from anywhere, and you'd be helping build something meaningful for SA businesses."

**If they're entrepreneurial:**
> "I know you've always been interested in startups. This is a chance to get involved early in something that's actually built and ready to launch."
