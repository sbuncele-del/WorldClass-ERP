## Protective Provisions

Investors holding 10%+ in aggregate shall have the right to approve:

1. Any amendment to the MOI affecting investor rights
2. Any increase in authorized share capital
3. Any new class of shares
4. Any liquidation, dissolution, or wind-up
5. Any merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets
6. Any transaction with related parties exceeding R100,000
7. Any change in the nature of the business

---

## Pre-Emptive Rights (Pro-Rata Rights)

Investors shall have the right, but not the obligation, to participate in future financing rounds to maintain their percentage ownership, on the same terms as new investors.

---

## Anti-Dilution Protection

**Weighted Average Anti-Dilution:** If the Company issues shares at a price below the price paid by Investors in this round (a "Down Round"), the Investors' conversion price shall be adjusted using the broad-based weighted average formula:

```
New Price = Old Price × [(Outstanding + New Money / Old Price) / (Outstanding + Shares Issued)]
```

**Exception:** This protection does not apply to:
- Employee stock option pool (up to 10%)
- Shares issued in strategic partnerships approved by the Board
- Shares issued in acquisition transactions

---

## Tag-Along Rights

If Founders propose to sell more than 25% of their shares to a third party, Investors shall have the right to participate in the sale on the same terms and conditions, pro-rata to their holdings.

---

## Drag-Along Rights

If shareholders holding 75% of the shares (including Founders) approve a sale of the Company, they may require all other shareholders to sell their shares on the same terms.

---

## Right of First Refusal (ROFR)

If any shareholder wishes to sell shares, they must first offer them to existing shareholders pro-rata at the same price and terms. Existing shareholders have 30 days to exercise this right.

---

## Liquidation Preference

| Term | Detail |
|:-----|:-------|
| **Type** | 1x Non-Participating |
| **Priority** | Investors receive 1x their investment before any distribution to other shareholders |
| **Participation** | Non-participating (investors choose preference OR pro-rata share, not both) |

**Example:**
- Investment: R2,000,000 for 16.67%
- Exit at R10M: Investors receive R2M (preference) OR R1.67M (pro-rata) → Take R2M
- Exit at R50M: Investors receive R2M (preference) OR R8.33M (pro-rata) → Take R8.33M
