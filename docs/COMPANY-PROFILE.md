## Key Differentiators

### 1. Native South African Compliance

Unlike global ERP vendors that require expensive localization projects (R500K-R2M), SiyaBusa has compliance built in from day one:

- PAYE, UIF, SDL tax calculations
- SARS submission file generation
- BCEA leave entitlements
- B-BBEE scorecard tracking
- POPIA data protection

### 2. Best Value Pricing

We are NOT the cheapest — we deliver MORE than basic accounting but cost far LESS than enterprise ERP.

| Market Tier | Monthly Cost | What You Get |
|:------------|:-------------|:-------------|
| Basic Accounting | R500-R1,500 | Accounting only |
| **SiyaBusa** | **R10K-R55K** | **Full ERP + compliance** |
| Enterprise ERP | R50K-R200K | Same features, 3-5x price |

### 3. AI-Powered Intelligence

9 specialized AI assistants provide:
- Natural language queries against business data
- Automated insights and recommendations
- Intelligent process automation
- 24/7 assistance

### 4. Modern Architecture

- Cloud-native SaaS (no on-premise infrastructure)
- Real-time data and dashboards
- Mobile-responsive design
- API-first for integrations
