## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1** In this Agreement, unless the context indicates otherwise:

**"Capital Stock"** means the ordinary shares of the Company, and includes any future class of shares;

**"Conversion Event"** means either: (a) an Equity Financing; (b) a Liquidity Event; or (c) a Dissolution Event;

**"Conversion Price"** means either: (a) the Safe Price; or (b) the Discount Price, whichever results in a greater number of shares for the Investor;

**"Discount Price"** means the price per share equal to the Equity Financing Price multiplied by the Discount Rate;

**"Discount Rate"** means **80%** (representing a **20% discount**);

**"Dissolution Event"** means: (a) a voluntary termination of operations; (b) a general assignment for the benefit of the Company's creditors; or (c) any other liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Company (excluding a Liquidity Event);

**"Equity Financing"** means a bona fide transaction or series of transactions with the principal purpose of raising capital, pursuant to which the Company issues and sells shares at a fixed valuation, including but not limited to, a Series A or similar financing round, resulting in aggregate gross proceeds to the Company of at least **R5,000,000** (Five Million Rand);

**"Equity Financing Price"** means the price per share paid by investors in the Equity Financing;

**"Investment Amount"** means **R________________** (_________________________ Rand);

**"Liquidity Event"** means: (a) a Change of Control (as defined below); or (b) an Initial Public Offering (IPO);

**"Change of Control"** means: (a) a merger, consolidation, or other corporate reorganization in which the Company's shareholders immediately before the transaction own less than 50% of the voting power after the transaction; (b) a sale of all or substantially all of the Company's assets; or (c) a sale of shares resulting in a change of more than 50% voting control;

**"Pro Rata Rights Agreement"** means an agreement providing the Investor the right to participate in future financing rounds;

**"Safe Price"** means the price per share equal to the Valuation Cap divided by the Company Capitalization;

**"Company Capitalization"** means all issued and outstanding shares of Capital Stock, assuming exercise or conversion of all outstanding options, warrants, and convertible securities (including all SAFEs), but excluding shares reserved for future issuance under equity incentive plans not yet granted;

**"Valuation Cap"** means **R15,000,000** (Fifteen Million Rand);

## 3. CONVERSION

**3.1 Equity Financing Conversion**

Upon the closing of an Equity Financing:

(a) This Agreement shall automatically convert into shares of the same class issued in the Equity Financing;

(b) The number of shares issued shall equal the Investment Amount divided by the Conversion Price;

(c) The Investor shall execute any documents required to effect such conversion.

**3.2 Liquidity Event Conversion**

Upon a Liquidity Event:

(a) If the Liquidity Event occurs before an Equity Financing, the Investor shall receive, at the Investor's election:

   (i) Cash payment equal to the Investment Amount; OR
   
   (ii) A number of shares of Capital Stock equal to the Investment Amount divided by the Safe Price, immediately prior to the Liquidity Event.

(b) If the Investor elects shares, such shares shall be issued immediately prior to the Liquidity Event and shall participate in the proceeds of the Liquidity Event.

**3.3 Dissolution Event**

Upon a Dissolution Event:

(a) The Company shall pay the Investor an amount equal to the Investment Amount from the Company's remaining assets;

(b) Such payment shall be made before any distribution to ordinary shareholders but after payment of the Company's debts and obligations;

(c) If the Company's assets are insufficient, payment shall be made pro rata among SAFE holders and other creditors of similar priority.

---

## 4. INVESTOR RIGHTS

**4.1 Information Rights**

The Company shall provide the Investor with:

(a) Monthly management accounts within **fifteen (15) days** of each month end;

(b) Annual audited financial statements within **ninety (90) days** of the financial year end;

(c) Notice of any material developments affecting the business within **seven (7) days** of such occurrence.

**4.2 Pro Rata Rights**

The Investor shall have the right, but not the obligation, to participate in subsequent Equity Financings on a pro rata basis to maintain the Investor's percentage ownership of the Company.

**4.3 Board Observer Rights**

The Investor shall have the right to appoint one (1) observer to attend meetings of the Company's board of directors in a non-voting capacity, subject to reasonable confidentiality requirements.

**4.4 Most Favored Nation**

If the Company issues any other SAFE with terms more favorable to that investor, the Company shall promptly notify the Investor and provide the option to amend this Agreement to include such more favorable terms.

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

**10.1 Governing Law**

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

**10.2 Dispute Resolution**

Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved as follows:

(a) The Parties shall first attempt to resolve the dispute through good faith negotiation;

(b) If negotiation fails within **thirty (30) days**, the dispute shall be referred to mediation;

(c) If mediation fails within **sixty (60) days**, either Party may refer the dispute to arbitration in accordance with the rules of the Arbitration Foundation of Southern Africa (AFSA).

**10.3 Entire Agreement**

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.

**10.4 Amendment**

This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

**10.5 Assignment**

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party, except that the Company may assign this Agreement in connection with a Change of Control.

**10.6 Notices**

All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by email (with confirmation of receipt) or registered post to the addresses set forth above.

**10.7 Severability**

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**10.8 No Partnership**

Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship between the Parties.

**10.9 Counterparts**

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
