**Usage Tips:**

1. **Personalize**: Always add 1-2 sentences specific to the recipient
2. **Keep short**: LinkedIn messages have character limits
3. **Clear CTA**: Always end with specific next step
4. **Follow up**: Don't give up after one message (3-touch rule)
5. **Track**: Use spreadsheet to track outreach and responses
6. **A/B test**: Try different subject lines and see what works
7. **Timing**: Send during business hours (9am-5pm SA time)
8. **LinkedIn posts**: Post 2-3x per week during launch phase
