**Usage Notes:**

1. **Personalize heavily** - Mention specific skills they have, past projects they've done
2. **Be authentic** - Don't oversell, just be honest about what you need
3. **Make it easy** - Specific asks, clear timelines, show you've done your homework
4. **Respect their time** - Make "no" an easy answer
5. **Offer value** - Money, equity, portfolio piece, future favors
6. **Follow through** - If they help, use their work prominently and give credit
7. **Stay humble** - You're asking a favor, not offering them an opportunity
