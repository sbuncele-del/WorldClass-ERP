# SiyaBusa ERP - Investor FAQ

### 📊 Business & Market

**Q: What is SiyaBusa ERP?**  
A: SiyaBusa ERP is a comprehensive, production-ready Enterprise Resource Planning system designed for South African and African businesses. We provide 25+ integrated business modules covering everything from financial accounting to industry-specific solutions for healthcare, mining, construction, and more.

**Q: What problem are you solving?**  
A: African SMEs and mid-market companies pay 10-50x more for ERPs than global competitors, often getting incomplete, inflexible systems. We provide enterprise-grade ERP at 1/10th the cost with built-in compliance for South African regulations (SARS, B-BBEE, etc.).

**Q: Who is your target customer?**  
A: Growing South African businesses (50-500 employees) across six key sectors:
- Healthcare providers
- Mining operations  
- Construction companies
- Property management
- Agriculture
- Professional services firms

**Q: What's your market size?**  
A: South African ERP market is ~$450M/year growing at 8.2% annually. Our addressable segment (mid-market) is ~$180M. Target 5% market share within 3 years = $9M ARR.
